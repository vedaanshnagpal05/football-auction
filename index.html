⚽ Top 5 League Auction

Bid for stars of the Premier League · La Liga · Serie A · Bundesliga · Ligue 1 — then simulate the season.

Clubs

2–6 clubs. Set each to Human (you / a friend) or AI.

Rules

How to play: players go under the hammer one by one. On your turn, Bid the next increment or Pass (passing is final for that lot). Fill up to 15 slots — you need at least 1 GK, 3 DEF, 3 MID, 1 FWD to field a proper XI. After the auction, the season is simulated: every club plays each other home & away. Strongest squad usually wins… usually. Keys: B = bid, P = pass.
Manual mode: perfect for a live auction with friends in the room — no turns or AI. You're the auctioneer: tap a club to record its bid, then hit 🔨 Sell (or Unsold). Budgets and squads are tracked for you; Human/AI toggles are ignored.
Draft mode: IPL-Unbeaten style — no money. Clubs snake-draft 12 rounds; on your pick you're dealt 5 random cards and choose one (keys 1–5). Unpicked cards go back in the deck.
